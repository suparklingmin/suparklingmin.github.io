I am **Sumin Park**, a computational linguist. I serve as a visiting assistant professor in the Data Science for the Humanities minor at the College of Humanities, Seoul National University, where I teach data science for the humanities.

I studied linguistics and mathematics at Seoul National University (BA, BSc) and earned my MA and PhD in linguistics there. My doctoral dissertation, *Fusion models for news quality prediction*, combined textual features with sentence embeddings.

## Research Interests

- Korean word and sentence embeddings and pretrained language models
- Natural language processing for medical and clinical text
- Quantitative lexical and semantic studies of historical Korean
- Teaching data science for humanities research
